## assetlinks.json Setup

After generating AAB from PWABuilder:
1. PWABuilder will provide SHA-256 fingerprint
2. Replace `__REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_SHA256_FINGERPRINT__` in
   `public/.well-known/assetlinks.json`
3. Deploy to Vercel
4. Verify: https://your-domain/.well-known/assetlinks.json

---

## Deployment Steps

1. Go to https://pwabuilder.com
2. Enter your Vercel URL
3. Click "Package for stores" → Android
4. Download AAB file
5. Go to https://play.google.com/console
6. Create new app → Upload AAB
7. Fill store listing (copy from above)
8. Upload screenshots + feature graphic
9. Complete content rating questionnaire
10. Submit for review (1~3 days)
